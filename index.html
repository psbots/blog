About me

I'm Praveen Sridhar, a technology enthusiast, developer and evangelist who loves to solve day-to-day problems with automation be it hardware or software. A mozillian at heart, I believe in the power of the Open Web and have been spreading the importance of Web Literacy with Mozilla Kerala. As a person who loves tinkering and making new stuff, I love learning and collaborating over ideas at TinkerHub, a Maker Space at CUSAT that I initiated with fellow geeks
